Where to Buy Fashionable Clothes In Divisoria

If you are looking for the best place to buy affordable products especially clothes or fabrics, there's no other place to go other than Divisoria. It’s true what they say that you’ll find almost everything you need there for the lowest price. There are many malls in divisoria, namely Tutuban, 168, 999, Lucky Chinatown Mall, Divisoria Mall, etc. But my favorite mall is 168, for it has many fashion boutiques of unique style of clothes. I go there several times a year, whenever my mom and I buys fabric for our uniform. As we visit Divisoria, the stores are becoming more fimiliar to me. Also, I have discovered my personal favorite shops for clothes. Here are someof my favorite stores:
1. Mura Dito Clothing

As the name itself says "Mura Dito", is the perfect word to describe this store. Variety of clothes are seen here perfect for your casual, street, summer, formal, and winter wears. It's an overrun stores with the famous brands such as Forever 21, H&M, Zara, Korean and Chinese brands. Also, there's a lot of imitation clothes here including Channel,  Fendi, Louis Vuitton, Gucci, etc. 

Affordability Rate: 4/5

Location: Tutuban Mall (near escalator)

2. Virus

If you are looking for winter clothes, then, this store is perfect for your finds. They have a lot of jackets, both denim and leather, sweaters, coats, trench coats, trousers, denim pants, etc. Aside from winter clothes they also have casual clothes perfect for everyday wear

3. Celest'

This store is perfect for you, if you are looking for clothes to wear on a special occasion such as weddings, baptismal, debuts, or even if you just feel like being fancy. They have cute and very girly clothes like Tweed skirts, vests, suits, dresses perfect for summer formal and casual. 

Affordability Rate: 3.5/5

4. K-Style Clothing

Korean style clothing is fast emerging in our country, for we adapt to their culture. It's no doubt that we are loving their style of clothes, because they are very cute. In this store, you don't have to spend money on plane ticket and travel to korea just to buy korean clothes. Skirts, cute tops, shorts, sweaters, etc. Also, it is the crowd's favorite for teenagers.

Affordability Rate: 4.5/5

Location: 168 Mall (3rd flooor, beside virus)
