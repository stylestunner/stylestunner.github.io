Everything About TayTay Tiangge

You’ve probably heard of the Taytay Tiangge at least once before, and you’ve probably been told that it’s enormous and insanely affordable. Taytay, dubbed as the Philippines’ Garments Capital, has taken over Youtube Philippines for quite some time now and there’s a good reason why. It’s just so amazing to see beautiful and good quality clothing at an insanely cheap price! There are a lot of tiangge centers to choose from, but BAGPI and IGPAI are the crowd's favorite. 
BAGPI

Location: BAGPI is in between MySeoul and Taytay Mega Tiangge (TMT).

Schedule:

Monday – 10:00 AM to 2:00 AM of the following day
Thursday – 10:00 AM to 2:00 AM of the following day

IGPAI

What You’ll Find
Ready-to-wear clothes are the heart of the Taytay Tiangge. They have everything— from the latest trends in women’s fashion, children’s pantulog and men’s sportswear. Items you see in online shops are usually available in Taytay too, and you’ll be amazed to see that Greenhills and Divisoria clothes are sold here for less. You can also find high-quality overruns for very low prices.  While most items in Taytay are “free size,” or in reality, made to fit small to medium frames, there is still a good selection for other body types. 

How Much You Should Expect to Spend
Tops can be bought for as low as P40-P100 apiece. Skirts and shorts are roughly the same range. Overruns can be worth as much as P300, while denims are more or less the same. Depending on the quality and design, which shouldn’t be hard to judge, prices may vary. What we can guarantee, though, is that they are much cheaper than items sold in Divisoria, Baclaran or Greenhills. A P400 top in Greenhills can be bought for P100 in Taytay.

More Pro-Shopper Tips
Taytay Tiangge is a marketplace, and you should be prepared to go into battle mode before you get there. 
1. Bring your own eco-bag. Expect to spend little, but shop a lot. Your own shopping bag means less plastic bags from vendors, and a more eco-friendly shopping experience!
2. Bring lots of change and small bills. 
3. Bring friends with you. You can get wholesale prices for 3-piece purchases, so having friends with the same taste and style will definitely help you save money.

4. Wear comfortable clothes. The tents will you make you feel like you're inside an oven so it's important to wear light clothes. Plus, there are no fitting rooms available.
